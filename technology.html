Technology should make coverage easier to verify.

Security buyers often struggle to know what happened after hours. A guard may have been scheduled, but the client still needs to understand patrol movement, site activity, incident timing, and whether conditions were communicated quickly.

Highline's technology story is strongest when it stays practical: GPS guard movement where appropriate, real-time client communication, portal access, reports, and a record that helps managers evaluate coverage without chasing updates.
